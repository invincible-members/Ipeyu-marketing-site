Refund Policy

Last updated: 24-10-2024

Welcome to IpeyU! This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions under which refunds will be processed for transactions made through IpeyU App

1. Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds may be issued under the following circumstances:

  • Failed Transactions: If a transaction fails, but the amount is debited from the user’s account.
  • Duplicate Payments: If the same amount is deducted more than once for the same transaction.
  • Unauthorized Transactions: If unauthorized transactions are reported by the user within 72 hours.
  • Merchant Refunds: For cancellations, returns, or adjustments processed directly by merchants.

Refunds will not be issued for:

  • Transactions authorized by the user where goods/services have been successfully delivered.
  • Transactions not compliant with the platform's terms of service.
  • Recharges or Payments Made to the Wrong Recipient: Refunds are not applicable if a recharge or payment is made to the wrong account due to user error.

2. Refund Process

A. For UPI Transactions

Refunds for failed or disputed UPI transactions will be processed according to NPCI (National Payments Corporation of India) guidelines. Processing may take 3-5 business days after confirmation of the failure or dispute.

B. For Wallet Transactions

If a wallet transaction fails but the amount is deducted, the refund will be credited back to the wallet within 48 hours. Users may transfer the refunded amount to a bank account, subject to standard transfer fees (if applicable).

C. For Merchant Payments (QR Code / Payment Gateway)

Refunds for merchant payments will follow the merchant’s refund policy. Users should check directly with the merchant regarding returns or cancellations. If a refund is initiated by the merchant, it will be credited back to the original payment source within 7-10 business days.

3. Refund Timelines

  • UPI Transactions: 3-5 business days.
  • Wallet Transactions: 2-3 business days for wallet refunds, 5-7 business days for bank account transfers.
  • Merchant Payments: Up to 10 business days, depending on the merchant's processing time.

4. Refund Mode

Refunds will be issued through the original payment method (e.g., UPI, wallet, bank account). No cash or alternate form of refund will be provided.

5. How to Request a Refund

Users can request a refund via:

  • In-App Support: Use the “Help” or “Support” section within the app.
  • Email Support: Contact us at [mailto:info@ipeyu.in] with the transaction details.
  • Phone Support: Call our customer support helpline at 8667858046.

6. Dispute Resolution

If you are unsatisfied with the resolution provided by our support team, you can escalate the issue through these channels:

  • Contact our Grievance Officer at sarathi.invincible@gmail.com within 30 days.
  • For UPI-related disputes, reach out to your bank or NPCI.

7. Cancellation Policy

Once a transaction is completed, it cannot be canceled. For mistaken payments or suspected fraud, contact customer support immediately.

8. Changes to This Refund Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Changes will take effect immediately upon posting. We encourage users to review this policy periodically.

For any questions or concerns regarding this Refund Policy, please contact us at [mailto:info@ipeyu.in].
